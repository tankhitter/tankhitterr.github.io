Artificial Intelligence

Need Of AI

Each team has their own tank to play the game. The server will start by the admin and maximum of five players can be able to join the game. Each player has to shoot other players and collect points in different ways. For that purposes, each tank should be able to control by theirselves. So the need of AI is very importnat. So that AI should have the ability to defeat other players and win. Those who have the best AI wil get all.

How to develop?

When consider about shooting to the oppponent, player has to think mainly two things. Firstly, is user player safe enough?. Secondly, can player shoot to the oponent well? If player can trust above two conditions then he can shoot the others. Basically to hide and shoot the opponent well we can use Min-Max algorithm. Visit wiki page from here. Another way to archive marks is getting coin piles. For that achivement player has to use the shortest path to reach them beacause coinpiles will disappear after sometime. In addition using a shortest path is very important to find health packs because they will also disappear after sometime. Hence player needs an algorithm to find the best coinpile using a best path. Some useful algorithms can be found from here.
