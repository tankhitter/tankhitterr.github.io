Basic rules for the Game

Introduction

The main objects of the game are battle tanks. This game is intended to be played by the multiple players. Each player is a client application created by different team. We, Team Tankhitter responsible for creating one of the cilent application. Each client application handling a particular tank in the game instance.

Objects

Bricks

Statics blocks of bricks can be seen in the user interface of the game. They are considered to be obstacles. Players cannot go through unless it is broken.

Water

Tank cannot go through water blocks. But you can shoot through them. Bullets will pass through water blocks.

Stones

Blocks of stones can be identified in the user interface of the game. They are considered as obstacles too. They cannot be destroyed by shooting or any other activity

Coin Packs

Coin packs will be appear on the screen. Players are suppose to collect those coins packs. Players can collect them by moving to the position of the coin pack before it dissaperas. Coin packs will automaticlly dissapere after its life time over. Coin packs will generate on screen due to two reasons. They are Random treasures or spoils of war.

Life Packs

Life packs will also appear on the screen. Players are suppose to collect those life packs. Players can collect them by moving to the position of the coin pack before it dissaperas. Life packs will automaticlly dissapere after its life time over. Collecting a life pack will increase the health of the player 20% of the initial value. It is possible to have a greater health than the health value at the begining of the game.

Breaking bricks

Player can breaks the brick blocks by shooting to them. Four shots (bullets) will be sufficient to destroy the a wall completely. After destroying, it will act as a normal blank block.

Shooting

Players can shoot bullets. If a bullet hit another players tank, its health will be reduced by 10% of the initial health. A tank is considered to be dead if it reach health 0%. Its collected coins will be droped at the point of death. But its coin count will remain unchanged. Droped coin pack can be collected by any other player. 25% percent of dead players points will be added to the killer. Droped coin pack will also act as a normal coin pack.

Death

A players death will occur due to some reasons.

1. Trying to cross a water block.
2. Reaching the health level to 0%.

When a player die because he try to go through a water block, no body will be credited since no body is a killer. In that case no coin packs will be droped by the dead plyer. But dead clients get their coin count set to zero nevertheless.

Penalties

If a client hits a brick block or a stone block, a fixed amount of points will be deducted from the clients. This will occur in each turn. If a client keeps hitting on such a obstacle, its points will be reducing continuously.
